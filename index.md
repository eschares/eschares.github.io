I am the **Engineering & Collection Analysis Librarian** at [Iowa State University](https://www.lib.iastate.edu/) in Ames, IA, and a **Research Associate** with the [ScholCommLab](https://www.scholcommlab.ca/) at the University of Ottawa, Canada.

I am also a **PhD candidate** at the Centre for Science and Technology Studies ([CWTS](https://www.universiteitleiden.nl/en/social-behavioural-sciences/cwts)), Leiden University, Netherlands under the supervision of Drs. Ludo Waltman and Thomas Franssen, expected 2030.

My research focuses on bibliometrics, scholarly communication, and open science. I have published about article processing charges [[1]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.16551), impacts of the 2022 OSTP memo [[2]](https://doi.org/10.1162/qss_a_00237), and making collection development decisions using the tool Unsub [[3]](https://doi.org/10.1162/qss_a_00200). 
I've also been an expert consultant to the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy [[4]](https://bidenwhitehouse.archives.gov/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Open-Access-Publishing-of-Scientific-Research.pdf) and participated in an NSF grant [[5]](https://investinopen.org/data-room/reasonable-costs/).

Prior to my current role at Iowa State, I worked at Intel for 10 years on NAND flash memory.
